"The twelve Caesars" by Suetonius

Seutonius (or Gaius Suetonius Tranquillus) was born circa A.D. 70 and died sometin after A.D. 130. He composed a history of the twelve Caesars from Julius to Domitian (died A.D. 96) His work was significant contribution to the best-selling novel and television series "I, Claudius." Suetonius' work includes biographies of the following Roman emperors:

  • Julis Caesar
  • Augustus
  • Tiberius
  • Gaius (Caligula)
  • Claudius
  • Nero
  • Galba
  • Otho
  • Vitellius
  • Vespasian
  • Titus
  • Domitian
