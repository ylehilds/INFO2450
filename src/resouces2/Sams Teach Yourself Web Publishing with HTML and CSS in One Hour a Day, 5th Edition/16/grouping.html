Chiroptera

Winged mammals (that is, bats) which include the following families:

Subspecies Representative Animals
Phyllostomidae Leaf-nosed and hog-nosed bats
Vespertilionidae Plain-nosed bats, including brown bats
Molossidae Free-tailed and mastiff bats

Carnivora

Meat-eating mammals, including the following families:

Subspecies Representative Animals
Canidae Foxes, wolves, dogs
Felidae Cats
Ursidae Bears, pandas
Hyaenidae Hyenas
Viverridae Mongoose, civet
Mustelidae Badger, polecat
