Web Site Evaluations

The Purpose of this Web site is to evaluate the web design of 3 distinctive web sites.

Bellow, there are 10 questions that I will answer for each web site, for web design evaluation purposes:

  1. What was your first impression of this site?
  2. How does this site organize the flow of information? (Do they have links on the left and text on the right? Some other scheme? No scheme?)
  3. Does the site structure make sense or does it confuse you? Why?
  4. Does the graphics/layout/multimedia enhance the site or detract from it? Why?
  5. How long did it take for the home page to load? Was the load time reasonable?
  6. Was the text color and size readable? If a background color or image was used, did it enhance or detract from the site?
  7. Did the site have a navigation bar or other navigation tool? Was the navigation well done?
  8. Did the links used on this site allow for easy forward and backward navigation?
  9. Did the site have a color scheme or look and feel that was repeated on all the pages?
  10. What does this site offer that was unusual or very well done?

Evaluating Web Site: www.rythospital.com/nanodocs

  1. My first impression was that Technology has come long ways to help out health issues.
  2. The web site had links mostly on the middle center of page. Also had some pictures of their product.
  3. The site structure makes sense to me, because it is straight forward on what they want users to see.
  4. The graphics really enhance the site because it gives the users the images of the NANODOCS.
  5. The page loaded reasonably fast, without any delay.
  6. The text was colored and the size was readable, some links on the middle top of site had colored background, that made the text not very readable.
  7. The site had a navigation bar on the right hand side that helped to navigate it. besides the bar there were links to jump to different pages.
  8. Yes the links really made the site easy to jump pages, either forward or backwards easily.
  9. Yes, The technology look and feel was repeated on all pages.
  10. The site offers different and interesting information that is unusual and hard to believe (for example: male pregnancy).

Evaluating Web Site: www.hl2.com

  1. My first impression of this site was that it was very dynamic and interactive as pertaining to users.
  2. The site organizes information interactively, meaning whatever you are interested in and click, that's the only information you will get. They had links with an image.
  3. At first it confused me because of the simplicity. After playing around with it, it made complete sense.
  4. The graphics definitely enhances the web site, because it gives a feel of interaction.
  5. The web site took quite a while to load in my opinion. I think that was due to the animation going on. If you have a slow computer that may bother users, due to taking too long to load a page.
  6. The text was colored in black, which it suits well since the whole background was white.
  7. The site did not have a navigation bar, but it did have an image that you can interact with. The only information you see is what you request.
  8. Yes, The web site links to go forward or backward was very easy and efficient.
  9. Yes, With the white background and black color text. That was repeated through the whole web site.
  10. This web site was very well done, because of the well interaction between the site and its users.

Evaluating Web Site: www.gotduck.com

  1. I was really impressed about all the animations going on.
  2. the links were mainly on the middle top of the web site. It had some links as well on pictures
  3. At first it confused me because of the talking duck interaction. After a minute navigating the site, it was very enjoyable due to many animations and pictures.
  4. The graphics were very well done and definitely enhances the web site tremendously.
  5. The site took a little while to load, due to the animations. If you have a slow computer that may bother users, due to taking too long to load a page.
  6. The text was colored and the style of many pages gives a feel of handwriting text. Size was readable. Images were used to enhance the text.
  7. The site had links on the middle top of page that easily gets you where you want to go. There were no navigation bars.
  8. Yes, The links were very well done. It was really easy to navigate back and forth.
  9. Yes, mainly it was a gray color background map through the whole web site.
  10. The animations were definitely a huge plus on this web site. You don't see this kind of web site everyday. This is very good Web Site in my opinion.
